Bowmenn makes businesses move faster for shippers, drivers, and truck owners.

Bowmenn is a tech-enabled last-mile logistics platform that connects truck owners with businesses and individuals to provide on-demand delivery of goods and household equipment conveniently from the shortest distance to the last - mile.

On our on-demand platform, you are never short of options( Trucks, Pickups, and Vans) for your moving and hauling needs.

Bowmenn places in your hands, the power to manage your delivery business and save time and money. You choose what’s best for your present needs-whether you are a manufacturer, agro-entrepreneur, or industrialist, Bowmenn provides you the means to connect to your customers and distributors across Nigeria and beyond faster, cheaper, and with Insurance.

Bowmenn is currently accessible across Nigeria and growing fast. Our proprietary technology includes mobile and web apps used for seamless delivery and moving experiences.

Mission

Our mission is to organize the world trucking & hauling system of all categories. To break the chain of middlemen, to bring the hauling industry and, shippers together using technology and make them more accessible and useful

Vision

Our Vision is to create and organize a technology-based trucking system for all market categories.
